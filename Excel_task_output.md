Tasks Performed on Sample-Superstore.csv :-
- Removing Duplicates.
- Formatting the order date columns from(mm/dd/yyyy) to (dd/mm/yyyy).
- Converting Sales Datatype (Text into Numbers).
- Converting Quality Datatype (Text into Numbers).
- Converting Discount Datatype (Text into Numbers).
- Cleaning Profit Column (Removing '-' sign).
- Converting Profit Datatype (Text into Numbers).

------------------Exploratory Data Analysis Tasks-----------------------

Summary Metrics
- Total Sales
Formula used "=SUM('Cleaned Data'!G2:G9995)"

- Total Profit
Formula used "=SUM('Cleaned Data'!J2:J9995)"

- Total Orders
Formula used "=COUNTA('Cleaned Data'!A2:A9995)"
- Average Order value
Formula used "=B1/B3"

- Total Quantity Sold
Formula used "=SUM('Cleaned Data'!H2:H9995)"
